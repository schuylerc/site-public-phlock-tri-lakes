Welcome to Phlock Of The Tri Lakes Social Club

Come join us in 2026 ... it's the only way to get the members-only information about future music lineups. 2025 was full with so many concerts and gatherings, and our Phlock continues to grow!!

Our Charity remains the Golden Corner Food Pantry ... members are encouraged to volunteer at the Pantry M-F and the 1st of the month Saturdays ... any time 8:30-12:30, no commitment to time or day(s).

We are continuing regular clean-ups of our adopted section of Highway 28 through the Bountyland area.

Phun Things We Do

  • Lake Floats with Trop Rock musicians
  • House Concerts
  • Monthly Phlockings (Meetings)
  • Jazz on the Alley in Seneca (Monthly Thursdays)
  • We Party With A Purpose (major charity party)
  • Monthly Phun outings like Mini Golf, Sunsets
  • Building a float for a holiday parade

AND SO MUCH MORE!!!
